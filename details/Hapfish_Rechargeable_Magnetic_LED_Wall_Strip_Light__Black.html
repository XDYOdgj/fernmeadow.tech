Hapfish Rechargeable LED Wall Strip Lights

Transform your space with the perfect blend of style and functionality. Hapfish LED wall strip lights are designed to offer versatile illumination that fits seamlessly into your lifestyle, whether you're decorating a bathroom, lighting up a mirror, or enhancing your workspace.

Featuring 70 high-brightness beads and a powerful 300lm output, these lights deliver clean, bright light without excessive glare. With adjustable brightness and three color temperature options (3000K, 4500K, 6000K), you can easily match the ambiance to your mood or the time of day. The dimmable feature adds a layer of personalization, making it simple to create the ideal lighting for any occasion.

These lights are ideal for under-cabinet or mirror lighting, and their wireless design ensures a clutter-free setup. A strong built-in magnet allows for quick and simple installation on metal surfaces, while the double-sided adhesive snap and screws provide extra stability for secure mounting. With everything from dimmable under cabinet lighting to remote control desk lamps, Hapfish magnetic LED wall strip lights are built to deliver both comfort and elegance. Perfect for DIY enthusiasts, they bring modern lighting solutions to your home with minimal effort and maximum impact.
